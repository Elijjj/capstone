About Us

Our Story

Fedici is a brand born from a sister's love. It is a momento for Fides, the owner's sister who passed a few years back. The owner wishes to share Fides' great love for cheesecakes and hopefully bring more smiles to those who experience it.

Our Vision

To continue being a premium cheesecake in the Philippines in the next three years

Our Mission

Our mission is to carry on the essence of celebrating no matter the challenges encountered in achieving that milestone.
