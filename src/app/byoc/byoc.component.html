Build-Your-Own Cake

Build Your Own Cake is the hottest offer of Fedici.ph that lets YOU customize a cake to your liking! If you’re curious about the taste of our Signature Full Strawberry Cheesecake with white crust instead of brown crust, you can try them here.

Apart from the offered cakes in our menu, we now allow the customers to choose the components of their cake – from the cheesecake base, size, toppings, up to the crust, or even a flower of your choice. You can freely choose from our six cheesecake bases, along with our two main crusts – the white and brown crusts. You can also choose the toppings you want, from strawberries, blueberries, almonds, brulee, mangoes, pistachios, white or milk chocolate. Mix and match to your heart’s desires with Fedici’s Build Your Own Cake!
